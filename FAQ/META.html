Zsh META-FAQ

06 June 2000
Zsh was originally written by Paul Falstad <pf@zsh.org>. Zsh is now maintained by the members of the zsh-workers mailing list <zsh-workers@math.gatech.edu>. The development is currently coordinated by Peter Stephenson (pws) <pws@zsh.org>. The coordinator can be contacted at <coordinator@zsh.org>, but matters relating to the code should generally go to the mailing list.

Zsh FAQ

Zsh has a list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) maintained by Peter Stephenson <pws@zsh.org>. The latest version can be found at any of the ftp sites above, or at http://www.zsh.org/FAQ/.
