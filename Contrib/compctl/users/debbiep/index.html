Functions contributed by Debbie Pickett

The compctl commands associated with these completion helper functions are in Debbie's contributed .zshrc

match-Xcolours
Completion of colour names for X11.
match-Xcursor
Completion of cursor for X11.
match-echotc
Completion for zsh's echotc builtin.
match-mail
Completion for mail folders.
match-man
Completion for the man command.
match-sched
Completion for zsh's sched builtin.
match-services
Completion for Internet services, from /etc/services.
match-taropts
Completion of options used by the tar command.
