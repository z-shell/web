Introduction to ZSH
  • A workshop on Zsh by Larry P. Schrof. This contains lots of information on the basic use of zsh; it's a good place to read about basic setup if the manual is too fustrating.
  • The UNIX FAQ has a document on shell differences which may tell you what you need.
  • Introduction to ZSH
    This is the HTML'ified introduction derived from the texinfo version created by Jonathan Hardwick <jch@cs.cmu.edu>. Beware that it is pretty old, though, and may not reflect the current state of zsh well. The PostScript of Paul Falstad's introduction is also available. The latter is not hypertext, but you can download and print it at your site, or view it with an external PostScript viewer.
